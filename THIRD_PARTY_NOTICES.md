## Cave Pony's independent work

Cave Pony is not a source-code fork of either project. Its skill wording, repository implementation, tests, documentation, and visual identity are independently authored.

Its main coordination additions include:

- independent `build` and `voice` controls;
- explicit activation and coexistence rules;
- one footprint ladder spanning no-change, deletion, reuse, platform, dependency, and local-code choices;
- a smallest-decisive-proof rule expanded by material risk;
- one compact `Done`, `Proof`, `Skipped`, and `Risk` report;
- a shared clarity override for destructive, security-sensitive, legal, financial, migration, and recovery work;
- a read-only audit mode covering both implementation footprint and attention cost;
- conflict-resolution rules that prevent implementation minimalism or prose compression from weakening correctness.

See [Origins and differences](docs/ORIGINS_AND_DIFFERENCES.md) for the behavioural comparison.
